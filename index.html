About Pipinong Pagkain


Pipino, in tagalog, is translated to "cucumber" in english. The title "Pipinong Pagkain" is frequently mistakenly translated to "Cucumber Food", but the real meaning of the title, which lies beneath the depths of formal translation, is "Pilipinong Pagkain." The inherent and sole aim of this website is to provide the masses with not only accessible Filipino recipes but also provide an opportunity for people to share recipies that they would like to push to the light of day.


What We Offer

  • An avenue to upload your own recipes
  • Filipino cuisine in two categories
  • Fact-checked and approved recipes of said Filipino cuisine

From ages 9 to 90 and medieval to industrial, Pipinong Pagkain is always here to serve. The country, the people, and most especially, your appetite.
