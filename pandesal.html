Pandesal

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk - lukewarm
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoon butter - melted (or margarine)
  • 1 ½ teaspoon instant dry yeast
  • ¼ cup breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Pour milk, beaten egg, and melted butter.
  3. Add instant dry yeast and fold until sticky dough forms, then knead dough into a ball ('till smooth and elastic) and lightly coat with oil. Then, let it rise.
  4. Punch down dough and divide to 2 equal parts, roll into a log, and cut each log into 6 smaller pieces. Then shape these pieces into balls and roll them into breadcrumbs.
  5. Let them rise again and then bake pandesal until sides are browned (preheat oven 185 C).
